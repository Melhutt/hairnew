    • WELCOME TO 'THE HOUSE OF HAIR'

    House of Hair is a modern unique Salon situated on the high street in Biggleswade. With over 50 years experience, the Team at House of Hair are Colour Experts and provide a premier bespoke colouring service.

    Our Team also regularly attend cutting and colouring courses in London to ensure you receive an exceptional and on trend hair cut and colour experience.

    With our wide range of haircare products we will be able to recommend the best products to prolong your colour and help you maintain your new style. We look forward to welcoming you to our Salon and providing a service that leaves you feeling Fabulous!
